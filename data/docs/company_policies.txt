COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

1. ATTENDANCE AND LEAVE POLICY

All employees are expected to arrive on time and follow the designated work schedule. 
We maintain flexible working hours while respecting core business hours (10 AM - 3 PM).

Annual Leave:
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Part-time employees receive pro-rata leave based on hours worked
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- During peak business periods, leave may be restricted

Sick Leave:
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year

Remote Work:
- Eligible employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Manager approval is required for remote work arrangements
- All company equipment and data must be secured appropriately

2. CODE OF CONDUCT

All employees must maintain professional conduct and respect workplace values.

Confidentiality:
- Employees must not disclose confidential company information
- All employee data must be handled according to data protection regulations
- Client information is strictly confidential

Anti-Harassment Policy:
- Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- All incidents must be reported to HR immediately
- Investigations will be conducted confidentially

Conflict of Interest:
- Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest
- Accepting gifts or bribes from clients is prohibited
- Side businesses must not compete with company interests

3. HEALTH AND SAFETY

The company is committed to maintaining a safe workplace.

Workplace Safety:
- All hazards must be reported immediately
- Safety equipment must be used as instructed
- Regular safety training is mandatory for all staff

Mental Health Support:
- Employee assistance programs are available
- Counseling services are provided confidentially
- Mental health days are supported

4. BENEFITS AND COMPENSATION

Salary and Bonuses:
- Salaries are reviewed annually
- Performance bonuses are awarded based on company performance
- Bonuses are paid in December (subject to company profitability)

Health Insurance:
- Company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage
- Coverage includes dental and vision care
- Family plans are available at group rates

Retirement Plan:
- Company contributes 5% of salary to retirement fund
- Vesting schedule: 20% per year over 5 years
- Employees can contribute additional amounts
